Pheid (pronounced /feed/)

Pick a handle, share it with your group, and everyone who subscribes gets your updates instantly. Built for coaches, landlords, teams, and communities who just need to get the word out.

Subscribe with a handle

Enter @handle to follow someone's main channel, or name@handle for a sub-channel. Subscribers are in instantly.

One message, everyone hears it

Send once; every subscriber gets a push notification. Focused, one-way broadcasts keep your updates clear and easy to act on.

Schedule ahead

Queue one-off or recurring messages — timezone-aware, perfect for practice reminders, rent notices, and weekly updates.

Why "Pheid"?

Named after Pheidippides, the ancient Greek messenger who ran from Marathon to Athens to deliver a single, urgent message. Pheid carries that same idea forward. Your group, notified instantly.
