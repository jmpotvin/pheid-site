Frequently Asked Questions

What is Pheid?

Pheid is a group notification app. Admins create "notifiers" and share a code. Subscribers join using the code and receive push notifications. No replies, no group chat — just one-way broadcasts.

How do I subscribe to a notifier?

Tap the subscribe button on the home screen, enter the code shared by the notifier's admin, and you're in. You'll start receiving notifications immediately.

How do I create a notifier?

Tap the create button on the home screen, give your notifier a name, and share the generated code with your group. Free accounts can create up to 3 notifiers.

Is subscribing free?

Yes. Subscribing to notifiers is always free with no limits.

What does Pro include?

Pro removes limits on notifiers, subscribers per notifier, and messages per month. It's available as a monthly ($4.99) or yearly ($49.99) subscription.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your device's app store:

  • iPhone/iPad: Settings > your name > Subscriptions > Pheid
  • Android: Google Play Store > Menu > Subscriptions > Pheid

You'll keep Pro access until the end of your current billing period.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings in the App and tap "Delete Account." This permanently removes all your data including notifiers, subscriptions, and messages. This action cannot be undone.

I'm not receiving notifications

Make sure notifications are enabled for Pheid in your device settings:

  • iPhone/iPad: Settings > Notifications > Pheid > Allow Notifications
  • Android: Settings > Apps > Pheid > Notifications

Some Android manufacturers (Samsung, Xiaomi, Huawei) have aggressive battery optimization that can delay notifications. Check your device's battery settings and exempt Pheid from optimization.

I need a refund

Refunds are processed by Apple or Google, not by us directly. Request a refund through:

  • Apple: reportaproblem.apple.com
  • Google Play: Google Play refund support
